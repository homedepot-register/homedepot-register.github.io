Home Depot Credit Card Payment Options

Home Depot offers multiple convenient ways to pay your credit card bill. You can choose the method that works best for you to ensure timely and secure payments.

  1. Home
  2. Card Payment Options
Activate Now
  • Online Payment: Log in to your HomeDepot.com/MyCard account, go to “Pay My Bill,” choose your payment method, enter the amount, and confirm the payment. Fast and secure.
  • Automatic Payments: Schedule monthly automatic payments from your bank account to never miss a due date. You can set the amount and payment date online.
  • Phone Payment: Call the Home Depot MyCard customer service number and follow the automated instructions or speak with a representative to make your payment over the phone.
  • In-Store Payment: Visit any Home Depot store and pay your MyCard bill at the customer service desk using cash, debit, or credit card.
  • Mail Payment: Send a check or money order along with your payment coupon to the address on your billing statement. Ensure it reaches before the due date.
