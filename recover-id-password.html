Recover Forgotten User ID or Password

If you forget your Home Depot MyCard username or password, don’t worry. You can quickly recover your account online and regain access securely.

  1. Home
  2. Recover ID Or Password
  • Visit the Official Website: Go to HomeDepot.com/MyCard and click on “Forgot Username/Password.”
  • Choose Recovery Option: Select whether you need to recover your username or reset your password.
  • Enter Required Information: Provide your Home Depot MyCard number, email address, or other personal details as requested to verify your identity.
  • Verify Your Identity: Complete the security verification, such as answering your security questions or entering a verification code sent via email or phone.
  • Create a New Password: If resetting, choose a strong new password with letters, numbers, and symbols to secure your account.
  • Confirm and Login: After verification, confirm the changes and log in using your recovered username or new password.
